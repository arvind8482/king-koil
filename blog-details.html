Best Mattress for Heavy People: Firmness, Thickness & Support

Home Blogs Blog Details

Firmness, Thickness
and Support Layers

Do you wake up with back pain despite sleeping 7-8 hours? Is your mattress sagging within months? If you weigh over 90-100 kg, finding the right mattress isn't just about comfort; it's essential for your health, spine alignment, and quality sleep.

This expert guide explains firmness levels, thickness, and robust support layers required for long-lasting comfort and spinal alignment.

Why Heavy People Need Specialised Mattresses

Standard mattresses designed for average body weights (60-80 kg) simply cannot provide adequate support for heavier individuals. Here's why specialised considerations matter:

Common Problems with Wrong Mattress Selection

Physical Issues

  • Chronic back, neck, and shoulder pain
  • Pressure point soreness at the hips and shoulders
  • Morning stiffness and reduced mobility
  • Body aches throughout the day

Sleep Quality Problems

  • Frequent nighttime awakenings
  • Difficulty finding comfortable positions
  • Overheating due to deep body impressions
  • Partner disturbance from motion transfer

Financial Concerns:

  • Premature sagging (often within 1-2 years)
  • Frequent replacements costing thousands
  • Frequent replacements costing thousands
  • Warranty claims denied due to "normal wear."
